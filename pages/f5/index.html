F5 : the security labs project

deployed
Problem Statement

F5 was having difficulty engaging and reaching the global security audience with their new F5 labs.

hypothesis and solution

Collaborate with the F5 labs security team to develop engaging digital experiences to tell their story.

key responsibilities

User research, visual design, development and UX.

Process and discovery

During discovery for these experiences for the F5 labs team, I developed a sitemap and IA around a security news focused platform. The security engineers I interviewed, spent a siginificant time of their keeping up to date reading security news websites and blogs, therefore a incident response driven platform may have worked for this team to develop credentials in this domain. However, the decision was made to develop smaller one-off experiences, opposed to a larger platform due to budget.
