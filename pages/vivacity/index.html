Please, take me back home

VivaCity : a hyper local experience discovery app

deployed
Problem Statement

Event listings are scattered on multiple platforms such as facebook, newspapers, blogs - making it difficult to find something to do in the moment.

hypothesis and solution

Develop a location-based mobile app that allows users to find an event based on their mood or discover events happening in their neighborhood.

key responsibilities

User research, feature definition, usability testing, visual design, and UX.

Process and discovery

Through early product discovery we decided based on the concept of our app, urban millenials would be the best target persona. I conducted research with millenials across Seattle to uncover what their biggest pain point surrounding event discovery was. The majority of the users I researched mentioned scroll fatigue - meaning that they were exhausted with having to scroll through listings on multiple platforms to find something relevant to them. For our MVP we decided on adding deep filtering, such as filtering by mood, neighborhood (for a hyber local experience) as well as time of day to reduce the listings and meet the user halfway. Following MVP we had intentions to integrate with their social networks such as Spotify and Facebook and use machine learning to provide a more curated list of events.
