The Prestige project : big data visualizations for your operations center

concept
Problem Statement

Enterprise operation teams have thousands of systems that require continuous monitoring and need a method to digest mass amounts of real-time system data.

hypothesis and solution

Develop a multi-platform monitoring application that enables operation teams to react quickly to system failures and pinpoint problem areas on a large scale.

key responsibilities

User research, prototype testing, feature definition, big data visualization, visual design, and UX.

Process and discovery

Operations teams primarily consist of men and spend countless hours in front of a screen. Speaking with our users, I uncovered that a significant portion of them are gamers. Therefore, I wanted to design with a dark aesthetic to allow ease on the eyes, and gamify the way we display and render mass amounts of system data which includes developing a scatter plot like system to view groups of correlated failures at once. The interactions map those of a computer gaming system as well, leveraging keyboard shortcuts to control speed, spin cycles, and zoom into failure points.
