Please, take me back home

Radish : love the food you have on hand

concept
Problem Statement

In most cases when you buy ingredients to make a specific recipe you end up with left over ingredients you never think you will use again, which leads to excess food waste.

hypothesis and solution

Leveraging AI for ingredient substitution, develop an application that allows users to find recipes with only the food they have on hand, with an overall goal to reduce food waste.

key responsibilities

Concept and concept validation, competitive analysis, monetization strategy, visual design and UX.

Process and discovery

The initial concept for Radish came from a personal pain point of never being able to use all the food I have purchased, or having to buy an entirely new set of ingredients to make a recipe I found online. I wanted the freedom to buy seasonal and interesting ingredients, and be able to cook a delicious meal within my busy schedule - without the cost of services such as Blue Apron. I took this concept and gathered similar pain points from my colleagues and friends, which later became Radish. I have presented Radish to investors and startup meetups throughout Seattle.
